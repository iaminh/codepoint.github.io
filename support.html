Support

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I place an order? Browse the menu, add items to your cart, and checkout.
  • How do I make a reservation? Go to Reservations, select your date and time, and confirm.
  • Can I modify my order? Contact support immediately and we'll do our best to help.
  • How do I reset my password? Use the login screen to request a password reset.

Common Issues

  • Order not received? Check your order history or contact support.
  • Payment failed? Verify your payment details and try again, or use a different method.
  • App not loading? Try closing and reopening the app, or reinstalling if needed.
